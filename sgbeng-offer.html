 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

 What is an E-Wallet? 

An E-Wallet is a virtual wallet that stores digital credits, allowing users to make online and in-store payments on platforms such as ShopeePay, GrabPay, and Razer Gold.

 What do you do with my E-Wallet? 

We purchase E-Wallet credits to facilitate fund transfers via PayNow. This service helps small businesses and provides low-income individuals with the opportunity to sell their E-Wallet credits in exchange for cash.

 Is selling my E-Wallet safe? 

Yes, selling E-Wallet credits is safe. E-Wallets are managed by trusted financial platforms like ShopeePay and GrabPay. They are not considered traditional bank accounts, and selling digital assets, such as gaming accounts, is legally permitted.

 Is selling my Bank Account safe? 

Yes, selling Bank Account is safe. Your Bank Account used for personal usage and surely we do not want risk the account to get frozen we will treat it with extreme care just like our own account!

 How does the process work? 

Simply click on any of the E-Wallet logos on our site to connect with our live support agent. They will guide you through the process of securely transferring your E-Wallet credits to us.

 When will I receive my payment? 

Payments are processed swiftly! For E- Wallets, once the transfer is complete, you will receive your cash within just one hour. For Bank Accounts, Payment will be processed within 24 Hours.
